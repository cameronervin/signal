Rep value
05 / 06

How this helps an SDR

Less research time. More qualified conversations.

Who first

The queue sorts by tier and score, so the rep starts with the highest-fit inbound lead.

What to say

Talking points and draft snippets are grounded in source facts the rep can review.

How urgent

Trigger events, market pressure, and tier SLAs make speed-to-lead visible.

Before Signal

  • Manual tabs and inconsistent lead research.
  • Low-quality records consume rep attention.
  • Personalization depends on rep bandwidth.

With Signal now

  • A/B/C triage focuses the work queue.
  • Gate-failed leads show no draft or send-like action.
  • Cited outreach gives reps a safer starting point.

Future state: digital worker handoff

  • Approved leads move to a gated worker for first interaction and follow-up.
  • SDRs focus on qualified replies, meetings, and closing work.
  • Human review stays in front of outbound messaging policy.
Decision support first, automation after trust is earned. Rep leverage

Rollout
06 / 06

Project plan

Augment first. Prove lift. Then automate.

Earn trust with reps and managers before expanding the worker into follow-up.

Week 1

Build and validate

Document rubric assumptions, test sample leads, review intake and outputs with SDR manager and RevOps.

Weeks 2-3

Shadow

No rep behavior change. Validate enrichment, calibrate tiers, and capture baseline metrics.

Weeks 4-7

Mixed SDR pilot

Run 3-5 reps: champions, skeptic, median performer, and ramping rep. Ship feedback weekly.

Weeks 8-10

Graduated rollout

Expand team-by-team. Pilot SDRs enable peers; RevOps takes the runbook and rubric config.

Q2

Calibrate and automate

Regress scores vs. conversion, recalibrate weights, scope human-approved worker follow-up.

Success criteria

  • A-tier speed-to-lead down 50%.
  • Why-line accuracy over 80%.
  • Response rate up; draft edits minimal.

Kill or rework

  • Scores conflict with rep judgment.
  • Personalization is unsupported.
  • Latency breaks speed-to-lead SLA.

Stakeholders

SDR manager RevOps Marketing Ops Sales leadership Security review
